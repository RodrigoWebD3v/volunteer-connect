# Feature Research: Volunteer Connect

## Table Stakes

- User registration and login.
- Role separation between volunteers, NGOs, and admins.
- NGO profiles.
- Opportunity listing and detail pages.
- Volunteer applications to opportunities.
- Basic status tracking for applications.
- Event or activity scheduling.
- Participation history.
- Clear empty states and simple navigation.

## Differentiators

- Engagement history for volunteers.
- NGO-side organization of activities, schedules, and volunteers.
- Trust signals around NGOs and opportunities.
- Accessible, low-friction onboarding for first-time volunteers.

## Anti-Features For v1

- Real-time chat.
- Donations/payments.
- Complex gamification.
- Algorithmic matching before enough data exists.
